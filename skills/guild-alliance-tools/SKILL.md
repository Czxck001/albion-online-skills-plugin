## 1. ZvZ Strategy

### What is ZvZ?
Large-scale open-world PvP between guilds/alliances. Typically 20–200+ players per side.
Victory depends on composition, discipline, positioning, and shot-caller skill.

### Current ZvZ Meta Healers (Abyssal Depths Patch 5, per Metabattle)
The three dominant ZvZ healer builds:
- **Blight Staff** — strong sustain HoT profile
- **Fallen Staff** — displacement + healing, excellent peel
- **Rampant Staff** — energy management + HoT stacking

All three use similar healer armor:
- Chest: **Cleric Robe** (healing amp) or **Mage Robe** (situational purge)
- Shoes: **Refreshing Sprint** (energy) or **Mistwalker** (mobility)
- Cape: **Lymhurst Cape** (mana efficiency — healer staple)
- Passive: Focus on healing power, cooldown reduction

### Standard ZvZ Composition

#### Frontline (Tank)
- **Role**: Absorb damage, land CC, create space for DPS burst
- **Weapons**: Polehammer (Groundbreaker range 20m after Patch 6 buff — can now stun
  through territory walls), Bedrock Mace, Oathkeepers, Soulscythe
- **Armor**: Full Plate (Royal Armor / Knight Armor for CC immunity W-slot)
- **Key ability**: Stalwart while casting, Juggernaut for unstoppable engage

#### DPS Layer
- **Role**: Nuke priority targets called by shot-caller immediately after tank CC lands
- **Weapons**: Brimstone Staff, Permafrost Prism, Boltcasters/Weeping Repeater,
  Glaive (Halberd now adds CC resistance reduction — useful utility DPS in Patch 3)
- **Armor**: Cloth or Leather depending on weapon
- **Key**: AoE burst windows — coordinate with healer team

#### Healer / Support
- **Role**: Keep frontline alive, cleanse debuffs, enable burst windows
- **Weapons**: Fallen Staff (peel), Blight Staff (sustain), Rampant Staff (energy)
  and Hallowfall (must-have in any comp)
- **Ratio**: 1 healer per 5–7 players minimum
- **Note**: Lifetouch Staff reworked in Patch 2 — lower burst heal but now grants
  +15% damage buff to target. Situationally powerful.

#### Utility / Bombers
- **Role**: Disrupt enemy burst, provide vision, zone control
- **Examples**: Ethereal Form bombers (invincibility nerfed 4.5s→4s in Patch 3 — punish
  within the 2s vulnerability window), Frost column, Nature debuffers

### ZvZ Caller Guidelines
- Call targets by guild/color — not player name
- Cycle: Tank CC → immediate DPS burst → reset and reposition
- Never split (tunnel vision = wipe)
- Voice comms discipline is mandatory — one caller, no noise

### Positioning Diagram
```
[Enemy Zerg]
      ↑

[Your DPS]        ← stacked, in healer range
[Your Healers]    ← behind DPS, protected by utility
[Logistics]       ← mounts + replacement gear on standby
```

## 2. GvG / Crystal Arena Composition

### What is GvG?
Scheduled 5v5 fights over territories. Individual skill and team coordination matter
more than numbers. Crystal Arena is the instanced 5v5 ranked mode.

### Standard GvG Archetypes

#### Burst Dive Comp
| Role | Weapon | Purpose |
|------|--------|---------|
| Tank/Initiator | Polehammer or Bedrock Mace | Open fight, land CC |
| Burst DPS 1 | Brimstone Staff | Nuke priority target |
| Burst DPS 2 | Permafrost Prism | Follow-up CC + nuke |
| Healer | Hallowfall | Sustain + cleanse |
| Flex/Roamer | Bloodletter or Dagger Pair | Cleanup + peel |

#### Attrition/Sustain Comp
| Role | Weapon | Purpose |
|------|--------|---------|
| Tank | Oathkeepers | Infinite sustain frontline |
| Offtank | Mace | Peel + secondary CC |
| Hybrid DPS | Rotcaller (Cursed) | DoT pressure + debuff |
| Healer 1 | Lifetouch Staff | +15% damage buff for DPS |
| Healer 2 | Blight Staff | HoT sustain |

### GvG Tips
- Run the comp 10+ times in practice before real fights
- Identify counter-comps during scrims and prepare answers
- Designate one shot-caller — final word during fights, no debate
- Review replays — small positioning errors lose fights

## 3. 2v2 Hellgate Strategy

2v2 Hellgates are one of the most skill-expressive activities in Albion — combining
PvE mob clearing with surprise 2v2 PvP encounters.

### Hellgate Basics
- **Access**: Hellgate Ritual (buy, loot from mobs, or craft at Artifact Foundry)
- **Modes**: Non-Lethal (yellow zone, practice) or Lethal (red/black zone, full loot)
- **IP Caps**:
  - Non-Lethal: min 800 Base IP, cap 900 (soft 90%)
  - Lethal: min 900 Base IP, cap 1100 (soft 80%)
- **Completion thresholds**: Non-Lethal = 100 infamy; Lethal = 500 infamy
- **Matchmaking**: Based on your team's average Hellgate Infamy (separate for 2v2 / 5v5 / 10v10)
- **Best base cities**: Caerleon (higher loot prices), Brecilien (cheaper gear/potions)

### Recommended Map Setup
Activate your map in Caerleon's **Lower Market** — this spawns the gate one zone away
from the city, minimizing travel and making chaining gates efficient.

### Lava Mechanic
When a second team enters your Hellgate:
1. Lava appears at map edges after **1:30 minutes**
2. First wave = warning damage (1% HP/sec, increasing by 1%/sec)
3. Second wave = drastically higher damage
4. Players in lava long enough gain **CC immunity** (prevents stun-lock wipes)
5. Lava pauses once it has enclosed a final central area — final fight zone

### Meta Compositions (2025)

**The standard: DPS + Healer** (most effective and consistent)

#### Top DPS Picks

| Weapon | Playstyle | Key Combo |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| **Dagger Pair** | Easiest entry. Stack 3 Q stacks, then W + E | With Fiend Cowl: auto → wait for healer Q → W before enemy healer's Blessing. With Mercenary Jacket: E first to prevent Blessing, then W + auto. Can bait experienced healers by canceling E (click away instantly after pressing E, or press S). |
| **Claymore** (Dual Swords) | Medium difficulty. Feyscale Robe burst. | Stack Q to 3, use Cultist Sandals, execute combo. With Purity Robe: stack on DPS first, then kidnap enemy healer. Ask your healer for Blessing before combo. |
| **Hellspawn** (Cursed) | Sustained DoT + tankiness. | Against Beam healers: use W2, focus healer. Hunter Jacket gives high defense vs. burst. Swap to Inferno Shield vs. one-shots or double DPS. |
| **Demonic** (Cursed Staff) | DoT separation comp. High mobility. | Focus double-stacking healer + DPS. Use W situationally with Cultist Sandals. E for burst and separating enemy pair. Use root W + sprint Sandals + Frost Shield vs. one-shot comps. Mage Robe = cheaper defensive alternative. |
| **Deathgivers** | Extremely mobile high-burst solo weapon. | Strong but facing ongoing nerfs; check current patch status before building. |

#### Healer Role Guide (2v2)

**Core Priority**: Use your Q spell off cooldown — this is your primary healing tool.

**W spell (Blessing)** usage:
- Use against enemy E spells (Dagger Pair, Claymore, Battle Axe)
- Against magic damage: combine with your E spell + bonus healing passive

**Spell-by-spell situational guide**:
| Ability | When to use |
|---------|------------|
| Holy Beam (Q) | Against Hellspawn or Sustain-over-Blessing comps. If enemy uses Hellspawn, use W2 then swap to last passive |
| Holy Orb | Use against Demonic if enemy doesn't run Purity Robe |
| Purity Robe (chest) | Displace enemy healers or use defensively. Vs. ranged: swap to Frost Shield. Use first passive. |
| Mage Robe (chest) | Purge Cultist Sandals or jackets/robes. Don't waste it purging stacks. Use first passive. |
| Royal Cowl (head) | Use every cooldown — maximize uptime. First passive. |
| Mistwalker Shoes | Use Afterimage vs. melee. Swap to Refreshing Sprint vs. ranged (unless Beam vs. Imp). Use Refreshing Sprint vs. double DPS and one-shot comps. Cooldown reduction passive. |
| Lymhurst Cape | Default — mana efficiency |

**Potions**:
- **Resistance Potion**: Kite after being purged twice in a row by enemy DPS
- **Cleanse Potion (T5)**: Counter stuns (dagger + grail comps). T7 potion vs. Demonic or Cursed combos
- **Energy Potions**: Against mana purge comps

**Positioning**: Always stay **behind your DPS** in front-to-back formation. Easier to
heal your DPS than yourself. Distance prevents most one-shots targeting you. Also helps
drop curse stacks naturally.

### Map Strategy
- **Open maps** (few pillars): Favored by Hellspawn and Demonic comps — open space
  for DoT kiting
- **Pillar/wall-heavy maps**: Favored by mobility comps (Bloodletter, Dagger Pair)
  for line-of-sight play
- If you're a DoT comp, **hold favorable maps** (don't rush to the next portal)

### Chaining Gates
After winning a Hellgate, an exit portal appears + another entry gate. Chaining lets
you go directly into the next gate without needing a new Ritual — very efficient for
grinding infamy and loot. Gear must still meet IP requirements.

### Progression Path
1. **Non-lethal** → Learn mechanics, get comfortable with your comp
2. **Low-infamy lethal** → First real fights; manageable opponent skill level
3. **High-infamy lethal** → Top-level competition; significant skill and coordination required

## 4. 5v5 / 10v10 Hellgate Strategy

- **10v10**: High-loot, large coordination requirement. Final boss drops significant
  Black Market loot. Use defined roles (2 tanks, 3 heals, 5 DPS is a common baseline).
  *Note*: 10v10 stats were archived in the Kill Stats UI in Patch 2, but content remains active.
- **5v5**: Often used by guilds for GvG/Crystal Arena practice. Treat it as a warmup
  5v5 fight — run your GvG comp and learn each other's timing.

**Lava mechanic** (all sizes): Same closing-lava behavior as 2v2. The lava starts
1:30 after the second team enters. Larger groups = more chaotic central engagements
once lava closes in.

## 5. Territory Management

### Income Sources
- **Resource nodes**: Passive silver/resources from labourers
- **Mob camps**: Farm for fame and loot
- **Hideout crafting stations**: Attract outside crafters (station fee income)
- **Territory bonuses**: Some territories have specific crafting bonuses

### Scheduling Recommendations
| Task | Frequency | Assigned To |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| Defend territory timers | Daily | Core PvP team |
| Labourer management | Daily | Any officer |
| Crafting station maintenance | Weekly | Economy officer |
| Income report | Weekly | Treasurer |

### Defense Checklist
- Know your attack timer window; have a response team online
- Pre-stage mounts and gear in hideout
- Assign a scout to spot incoming enemies early

## 8. Patch Note Analysis

When a player shares patch notes or asks about a recent patch:
1. Identify which weapons, armor, or mechanics changed
2. Cross-reference against the guild's comp and farming activities
3. Flag: buffs (opportunity), nerfs (adapt), neutral (monitor)
4. End with **concrete action items** — not just analysis

**Recent notable changes (Abyssal Depths series)**:
- Polehammer Groundbreaker range: 16m → 20m (Patch 6) — consider adding to ZvZ frontline
- Mistwalker Shoes dash: 11m → 10m (Patch 3) — affects ZvZ bombers relying on it
- Ethereal Form invincibility: 4.5s → 4s (Patch 3) — bombers more punishable
- Lifetouch Staff: lower burst heal, added +15% ally damage buff (Patch 2)
- Halberd: now reduces enemy CC resistance (Patch 3) — potential utility DPS pick
- Heavy Crossbow Sweeping Bolt: damage buffed (Patch 3) — potential ZvZ DPS option
