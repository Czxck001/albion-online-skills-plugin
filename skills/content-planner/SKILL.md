## Content Categories

### 🏰 PvE Content

#### Solo Dungeons
- **Best for**: T5–T8 solo players, fame + silver farming
- **How it works**: Random glowing portals spawn in open-world zones. Mob difficulty
  scales with dungeon tier. Clear rooms, loot chest at the end.
- **Risk**: Low (instanced), but exit can be contested in open world
- **Silver yield**: 300k–3M+ SPH depending on tier and IP
- **Fame yield**: Excellent per hour
- **Tip**: Use a fast, high-damage build. Always carry food + pots. Check zone hostility
  before entering (player count shown on minimap).

#### Corrupted Dungeons
- **Best for**: Solo PvP-oriented players, T5+ gear, 1v1 specialists
- **How it works**: Enter a corrupted dungeon portal. Another solo player can
  invade — you can accept a consensual 1v1 duel or attempt to escape.
  Closing zone (lava) forces confrontation if both players stall.
- **Tiers**: Rated by Hellfire rating (1–20+). Matchmaking is based on this rating.
- **Risk**: Medium — full-loot death inside. Gear loss if you die.
- **Silver yield**: 600k–2M SPH (higher with PvP kills)
- **Tip**: **Book Chests have been removed** from Corrupted Dungeons as of Patch 2.
  All treasure spawns are now Treasure Chests. Bring a mobile build; know your 1v1 matchup.

#### Group Dungeons
- **Best for**: Groups of 2–5, steady fame + silver
- **How it works**: Instanced dungeons scaled for groups. Boss at the end.
- **Risk**: Low (instanced); transport risk when exiting
- **Silver yield**: 800k–4M SPH split among group

#### Avalonian Dungeons
- **Best for**: Coordinated groups of 5–20, high-end PvE
- **How it works**: High-difficulty PvE content, requires specific roles and
  coordination. Found via Roads of Avalon or open world.
- **Risk**: PvP risk after clearing (transport)
- **Silver yield**: High, great for organized guilds

### ⚔️ PvP Content

#### 🆕 The Depths (Abyssal Depths Update, June 2025)
The Depths are Albion's newest content type — a fast-paced instanced dungeon
introducing **Orange PvP**, a new ruleset between Yellow and Red zones.

**Access**: Find **Antiquarian's Dens** — red glowing cracks in walls near the
Conquerors Hall in every Royal City. In Caerleon, it's a walkway into The Aqueducts.
Entrances open periodically in waves.

**Team size**: 2 or 3 players (solo entry also possible)

**Orange PvP Rules** (unique to The Depths):
- ✅ **Equipped gear is safe** on death (weapons, armor, accessories)
- ❌ **Inventory items drop** on death (loot, consumables, gathered resources)
- Gear takes durability damage equivalent to a Yellow Zone knockdown
- Killed players drop a **Soul** that others can collect

**How it works**:
1. Enter via Antiquarian's Den → matchmaking pools players from different Dens
2. Inside: fight mobs room by room. Clearing a room unlocks loot or buffs.
3. Three dungeon levels — going deeper increases risk and reward
4. **Collapse mechanic**: After a timer, the dungeon starts collapsing floor by floor.
   Players still inside when it collapses (or KO'd during collapse) are killed.
5. **60-second exit window**: Once the Treasure Vault is unlocked, an exit portal
   opens for 60 seconds. Make it out before time runs out.
6. **Soul system**: Dead players drop a Soul. You can choose to leave (Soul becomes
   Neutral — anyone can grab it) or wait to be revived by teammates.

**Why play The Depths**:
- Best PvP training ground — test expensive builds without risking them
- Learn combat mechanics with safety net
- Over 4 million runs completed in first weeks after launch
- Earn **Favor** to spend at the **Antiquarian NPC** (new spider mounts, cosmetics)

**Risk**: Medium (inventory loss on death, gear is safe)
**Silver yield**: 400k–1M SPH (from bag loot only — lower than red zone content)
**Recommended for**: New PvP players, build testing, casual daily content

#### 5v5 & 10v10 Hellgates
- **5v5**: Tactical fights requiring proper team comp; often used by guilds to
  practice GvG and Crystal Arena. IP cap: 1200 (soft cap at 1000 base IP req).
- **10v10**: Large-scale fights with high loot potential; requires strong coordination.
  Stats were archived from player profile UI in Patch 2, but content is active.
- **Lava mechanic**: When two teams enter the same gate, a rising lava wall closes in
  from the edges, forcing engagement. First wave = warning damage; second = lethal.
- **Reward**: Both mobs and boss/chest loot. Killing the enemy team yields their gear.

#### Corrupted Dungeons (1v1 PvP)
Covered above under PvE — unique hybrid content with 1v1 invasion mechanic.

#### Faction Warfare
- **How it works**: Join a Royal City faction. Fight enemy-faction flagged players
  anywhere on the Royal Continent. Earn **Faction Points** redeemable for unique
  mounts, capes, and gear.
- **Factions**: Caerleon, Lymhurst, Bridgewatch, Martlock, Thetford, Fort Sterling,
  Brecilien
- **Risk**: Medium — you're flagged only against enemy factions, not all players
- **Silver yield**: Faction points → valuable cosmetics and gear sold on market
- **Tip**: Head into enemy faction territory and contest outposts for bonus point gain.
  *Note*: As of Abyssal Depths, players retain their Faction Flag after Arena and
  Expeditions — no need to re-flag after instanced content.

#### Open World PvP (Outlands / Mists)
- **Outlands**: Full-loot, guild-controlled territory. Best rewards, highest risk.
- **Mists**: Solo/duo instanced zone with mobs, mini-dungeons, and player encounters.

### 📖 2v2 Hellgate Deep Dive

*(See the dedicated 2v2 Hellgate section in the Guild & Alliance Tools skill and the
Content Planner below for full build recommendations. This section covers mechanics.)*

**What it is**: A 2-player PvP/PvE hybrid dungeon entered via a Hellgate Ritual.
Two teams of 2 can encounter each other inside.

**Access**: Use a **Hellgate Ritual** — crafted at the Artifact Foundry, looted from
mobs, or purchased on the market. Activate the ritual in the correct zone type.

**IP Requirements**:
| Mode | Minimum Base IP | IP Cap |
|------|----------------|--------|
| Non-Lethal (yellow zone) | 800 | 900 (soft cap, 90%) |
| Lethal (red/black zone) | 900 | 1100 (soft cap, 80%) |

Soft cap formula: `Effective IP = Cap + (Excess × Soft Cap %)`.
Example: 1200 IP player in Lethal = `1100 + (100 × 0.2)` = **1120 IP**.

**Completion**: Kill enough mobs to reach the infamy threshold:
- Non-Lethal: 100 infamy points
- Lethal: 500 infamy points

**Best cities to start from**:
- **Caerleon**: Higher loot sell prices
- **Brecilien**: Cheaper gear and consumables (lower overhead)

**Lava mechanic**: If a second team enters your gate, lava rises from the map edges
toward the center after 1:30, forcing both teams to fight. Players who have been
in lava long enough gain CC immunity (to prevent stun-lock deaths).

**Reward**: Chest with Black Market loot. Killing the enemy team also drops their gear.
Chain Hellgates by entering the exit gate inside (if IP requirements still met).

**Infamy system**: Matchmaking is based on your team's average Hellgate Infamy.
Win → gain infamy → face stronger teams. Lose → lose infamy → face easier teams.

### 🌿 Gathering

#### How Gathering Works
- Gather raw resources from open-world nodes (wood, ore, fiber, stone, hide)
- Nodes are tiered T2–T8, appearing in corresponding biome zones
- Gathering fame unlocks higher-tier gathering on the Destiny Board

#### Zone Risk vs. Reward
| Zone | Risk | Reward |
|------|------|--------|
| Blue (Safe) | None | Low-tier nodes only (T2–T4) |
| Yellow (Contested) | Flagged PvP only | T4–T5 nodes |
| Red (Dangerous) | Full loot PvP | T5–T7 nodes |
| Black (Outlands) | Full loot PvP | T7–T8 nodes, dense and lucrative |

**Blue/Yellow zone tip**: As of Abyssal Depths, Blue and Yellow zones now have a
soft IP cap for open-world content — lower-tier gear is competitive here.

#### Biome → Resource Mapping
| Biome | Primary Resources |
|-------|-----------------|
| Forest | Logs, Fiber |
| Highland | Ore, Stone |
| Swamp | Hide, Logs |
| Mountain | Ore, Stone |
| Steppe | Hide, Fiber |

#### Gathering Route Tips
1. Use gathering gear specific to your resource type
2. Carry a fast escape mount (Swiftclaw, Direwolf) in red/black zones
3. Sell raw resources locally or refine for higher margins
4. In black zones, always have an exit plan (route to portal or hideout)

### 🗺️ Exploration Content

#### Roads of Avalon
- **How it works**: Hidden roads accessible through open-world portals.
  Lead to static dungeons, unique mobs, Avalonian Dungeons, and hideouts.
- **Risk**: Medium to High (full-loot in most roads)
- **Silver yield**: 1M–5M+ SPH for experienced groups

#### The Mists
- **How it works**: Solo/duo instanced zone with mobs and player encounters.
  Accessible via Mist portals throughout the world.
- **Risk**: Medium — encounters with other players possible
- **Silver yield**: 600k–1.5M SPH
- **Group dungeon entrances** can also be found in the Mists (patched in 2025)
